FAQ's

Answers to common questions.

How long does a typical organizing session take?

Most sessions run between 3-6 hours depending on the space and your goals. During your consultation, we recommend a realistic timeline before any work begins.

Do I need to buy bins and containers before we start?

No. We start with what you already own, then recommend only the products that improve function and fit your style.

Can you help with decluttering decisions?

Yes. We guide you through a supportive, judgment-free process to help you decide what to keep, donate, recycle, or discard.

Do you offer virtual organizing?

Absolutely. Virtual sessions include planning guidance, live organizing support, and custom action checklists.

What areas do you serve?

In-person services are available in your local region, with virtual support available anywhere. Contact us with your ZIP code for details.
